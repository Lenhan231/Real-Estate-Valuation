Register content of Capstone Project (*) 3.1. Capstone Project name:
English: Automated Real Estate Valuation and Market Trend Analysis
Vietnamese: Định giá bất động sản tự động và phân tích xu hướng thị trường  
Context:
The Vietnamese real estate market, particularly in primary urban centers like Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City, experiences rapid fluctuations and complex pricing dynamics. For instance, condominium prices in Hanoi recently saw average secondary prices rise by 26%. Traditional property valuation relies heavily on manual appraisals, which are time-consuming, subjective, and struggle to scale across massive property portfolios. An automated, data-driven valuation model provides buyers, sellers, and financial institutions with instantaneous, objective property price estimations based on historical data and geospatial features.
Objectives:
The objective is to develop a accurate AI pipeline capable of predicting the total price and price-per-square-meter of urban properties. The target technical milestone is to achieve a Mean Absolute Percentage Error (MAPE) of less than 10% on unseen test data. Additionally, the team must deploy a Business Intelligence dashboard that visualizes price heatmaps and market trends dynamically.
