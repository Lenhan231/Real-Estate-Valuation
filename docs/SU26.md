## 3. Capstone Project Registration Content

### 3.1. Capstone Project Name

- **English:** Automated Real Estate Valuation and Market Trend Analysis
- **Vietnamese:** Định giá bất động sản tự động và phân tích xu hướng thị trường

### Context

The Vietnamese real estate market, particularly in primary urban centers like Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City, experiences rapid fluctuations and complex pricing dynamics. For instance, condominium prices in Hanoi recently saw average secondary prices rise by 26%.

Traditional property valuation relies heavily on manual appraisals, which are time-consuming, subjective, and difficult to scale across massive property portfolios. An automated, data-driven valuation model provides buyers, sellers, and financial institutions with instantaneous, objective property price estimations based on historical data and geospatial features.

### Objectives

The objective is to develop an accurate AI pipeline capable of predicting the total price and price per square meter of urban properties. The target technical milestone is to achieve a Mean Absolute Percentage Error (MAPE) of less than 10% on unseen test data.

Additionally, the team must deploy a Business Intelligence dashboard that visualizes price heatmaps and market trends dynamically.

### 3.2. Main Proposal Content

#### Theory and Practice

- Data ingestion, cleaning, and construction of a preprocessing pipeline to handle outliers and format geospatial coordinates.
- Research into ML/DL architectures for tabular data processing and analysis.
- Research into Explainable AI (XAI) techniques.
- Research into public datasets, including:
  - Ho Chi Minh City Real Estate Data 2025
  - Vietnam Housing Dataset 2024
- Collection of additional data if necessary.
- Research into techniques for maintaining AI models in production.
